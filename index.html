So who is twenty one pilots?

twenty one pilots (often styled as tøp or TØP) is an American musical duo from Columbus, Ohio, consisting of Tyler Joseph and Josh Dun.
The duo's music incorporates elements of various genres, including rock, hip hop, and electronic.
